City of Albuquerque Resource

Free City
Construction Plans.
We Build Them.

The City of Albuquerque offers 12 pre-approved ADU construction plans — donated by local architects and already meeting current building codes. Download any plan free of charge and bring it straight to us for a build quote.

Plans come in four sizes (450–750 sq ft), three roof styles, and include a 3D-printable concrete option for each size. Flat-roof plans are permit-ready as-is; pitched-roof plans require a stamped structural drawing from a licensed NM engineer — which we can arrange.

Already have one of these plans? Skip the design phase entirely — bring us your city plan and we'll provide a fixed-price construction bid within 48 hours.

The Process

From Lot to
Move-In.

We manage every phase — design, permits, site prep, construction, and final inspection. Most ABQ ADUs complete in 90 days from signed contract.

01
Site Assessment
We visit your property, measure the lot, check setbacks, verify utility connections, and confirm zoning eligibility under Albuquerque's ADU ordinance. No charge.
02
Design & Bid
You choose a model. We customize dimensions, finishes, and placement. You receive a fixed-price bid — materials, labor, and permit fees included. No surprises.
03
Permitting
We submit the permit application to Albuquerque Development Services on your behalf and track approval. Typical approval: 4–8 weeks. We handle all revisions.
04
Construction
Licensed trade crews handle every phase — foundation, framing, MEP, insulation, drywall, and finish. Weekly updates. Weather-managed scheduling via SMS.

Is an ADU Right For You?

What You
Need to
Qualify.

Lot Size ≥ 5,000 sq ft
Albuquerque requires a minimum lot size for ADU eligibility. We verify this on our first visit — no charge.
Owner-Occupied Primary Home
You must live in the primary residence on the lot under current Albuquerque ADU rules.
Adequate Setbacks Available
ADUs require 3 ft rear and side setbacks in most zones. Our site visit confirms placement options before you commit.
Clear Title & No HOA Prohibition
We help you verify HOA CC&Rs and title conditions before any costs are incurred.
