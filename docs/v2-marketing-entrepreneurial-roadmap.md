# ABQ ADU V2 Marketing + Entrepreneurial Roadmap

This note separates the homeowner-facing website from the future builder-facing app. The website should sell trust, family value, design confidence, and next-step clarity. The builder app should become the operational system of record after the website update is complete.

## Already Represented

- Family-centered ADU positioning for multigenerational households.
- Five model starting points with square-footage clarity.
- City of Albuquerque free-plan pathway with 450, 550, 650, and 750 square-foot variants.
- Plans-to-Lowe's material bidout direction.
- Contractor advertising / subcontractor marketplace idea.
- Smart-home thermostat cross-sell direction.
- Weather-aware schedule messaging for subcontractors.
- Rental-income framing for ADUs.
- Future New Mexico property-tax appeal business idea.

## Missing or Underdeveloped Website Ideas

- Financing prequalification: add a homeowner-friendly "Can I afford this ADU?" path with lenders, credit unions, and payment estimates.
- Revenue calculator: estimate long-term rental, Balloon Fiesta, Zozobra, Santa Fe event, and hybrid family/rental income scenarios.
- Neighborhood targeting: add New Mexico zip-code landing pages for high-intent areas such as 87122, 87111, 87043, 87048, 87501, 87506, 87508, 87544, and 87547.
- Homeowner readiness score: panel capacity, sewer/macerator, setbacks, access, budget, family goals, and engineering complexity.
- Engineering-burden explainer: windows/openings larger than 3 feet, sliding-door removal, extra windows, roof changes, and structural stamps.
- Trust proof: client-ready sample bid packet, sample pre-construction contract, sample schedule, and before/after exterior finish gallery.
- Partner marketplace: sponsored lender spots, contractor profiles, supplier offers, smart-home upgrades, and referral tracking.
- Seasonal urgency: weather/rain/wind delay education and "start before monsoon season" calls to action.

## Builder App Ideas for Later

- Clerk + Supabase login, organizations, teams, role permissions, and project ownership.
- Lead-to-site-visit-to-bid pipeline with a 30-hour bid target.
- Bid engine with price per square foot, COGS per square foot, margin, allowances, alternates, and exclusions.
- Blueprint/material takeoff tool that starts with standard model assemblies and later supports uploaded plans.
- Supplier bid package export for Lowe's or other material desks.
- Signed pre-construction contracts, subcontractor agreements, work orders, SMS schedule updates, and weather-delay make-up plans.
- Homeowner communication preferences: best contact method, best time to reach, and project-specific notes.
- Future agent workflow for plan interpretation, bid drafting, engineering flags, and schedule recovery.

## Monetization Channels

- Lender/credit union sponsored placements and referral fees.
- Contractor advertising profiles for electricians, plumbers, framers, concrete, HVAC, roofing, and finish trades.
- Smart-home thermostat and energy-efficiency add-ons.
- Supplier referral or quoting partnerships.
- Rental-income planning upsell for homeowners.
- New Mexico property-tax appeal service with a success fee, proposed at 35% of verified first-year tax savings.
